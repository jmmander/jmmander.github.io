Hi there!

Welcome to my website! Make yourself at home, click around, check out the source code and see some of my favourite projects.

My name is Jacqueline Mander. I'm a software engineer with a passion for building software with an injection of fun and creativity. Hailing from the vast shores of Australia, following a brief interlude in China, I have been based in the USA since 2019.

If you want to chat about why Python syntax is the best thing ever or discuss a job opportunity, you can reach me through my LinkedIn or just email me directly!
